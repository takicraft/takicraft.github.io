How to Play Taki's Tap Tango Solitaire

  • Do you know how to play Solitaire? It is also known as Klondike in some countries. Anyway, the rules for Taki's Solitaire are similar to that game
  • Instead of standard playing cards, this game uses Word Cards and Category Cards. You cannot tell them apart when they are face-down, but once flipped face-up, the Category Cards are the ones with a yellow border.
  • The face-down pile in the top left is the "Deck". If you run out of moves, draw a card from here.
  • The top right is the "Category Area". You can place Category Cards into the empty spaces here.
  • The bottom section is the area called the "Tableau". You can stack cards that belong to the same category. If the card at the very front of a stack is face-down, tap it to flip it face-up.
  • To clear a category, you need to stack all of its matching Word Cards on top of the corresponding Category Card in the top right Category Area.
  • Clear all the cards to win the game!
